About Us

At Blessed Labs, we're pushing the boundaries of AI to create tools that don't just innovate, they transform. Founded by Eric Nelson, a proud member of the federally recognized Rappahannock tribe of Virginia and an AI specialist, we blend respect for heritage with a passion for building technology that powers progress. Our solutions include customized machine learning models and Game AI Infrastructure & Intelligent Systems for game developers, delivering advanced NPCs and ML systems that enhance gameplay and player engagement. We help businesses thrive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape while transforming gaming experiences and unlocking new possibilities.

Beyond technology, we're committed to impact. At Blessed Labs, we craft powerful AI tools while empowering our clients with the knowledge to leverage them effectively through practical education and resources. We're dedicated to making advanced technology meaningful, accessible, and capable of transforming industries because we believe that when AI is developed thoughtfully, it becomes a force for positive change.
